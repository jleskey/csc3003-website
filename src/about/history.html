Our Story

Our earliest founders, regrettably, failed to record our ealiest history. Acccording to legend, however, our our brand was forged in the creative fervor of the Bithagen community over forty years ago. Accounts differ as to the founding members of our small company, but we know for sure that celebrated game developer Prudence Gnuford and world famous author and filmmaker Sigurd Inta were among their number. Mr. Inta served in several executive positions and mentioned us most favorably in his manifesto before he went to live in the woods as a true naturalist. Ms. Gnuford still serves today as the chair for our board of directors. We have asked her many times for a more complete account of our history, but she maintains that she is not at liberty to say.

Legendary or not, to this day we stay true to our roots and look forward to many more years of relentlessly doing what we do, helping to bring stories into the world.
